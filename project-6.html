Unlucky ★ Chicken 44

Catch All 44 Chickens Before Time Runs Out

A casual PC game combining point-and-click searching with top-down RPG exploration. Manage seeds, feed, coins, and energy while retrieving 44 runaway chickens scattered across the land.

Project Type

PC Game

Role

Core Gameplay Developer

View Project Details

Project Overview

Unlucky ★ Chicken 44 is inspired by point-and-click searching games and the cozy RPG style of Cat Cafe 101. Players explore multiple farms to retrieve escaped chickens while managing resources and time pressure.

The gameplay emphasizes exploration, strategic spending, and energy management — especially during nighttime when energy drains continuously.

Key Features

  • Resource-Based Capturing: Capture chickens using seeds, feed, or coins depending on chicken type and location.
  • Day–Night Cycle: Nighttime increases pressure through continuous energy drain.
  • Energy System: Energy decreases when catching chickens and drains at night, requiring players to sleep to recover.
  • Exploration & Collection: Find seeds while exploring the world to continue your search.
  • Scoring by Days: Finish in fewer days to achieve a higher score.

Contribution (My Work)

  • Movement System: Implemented player movement.
  • Day–Night Cycle: Implemented time progression logic.
  • Chicken Logic: Programmed chicken capture and escape behavior.
  • Collection System: Built item and resource collection logic.
  • Recovery System: Implemented sleep-based recovery mechanics.
  • Energy Bar: Programmed energy bar logic and drain conditions.
