D'Classics Books

A Mobile Application for Classic Literature

An elegant app for book lovers to browse, manage, and order physical copies of curated classic books, featuring seamless navigation and a user-friendly ordering process.

Project Type

Mobile Application (Android)

Role

Lead Developer & UX Designer

View Project Details

Project Overview

Dâ€™Classics Books is an elegant and intuitive mobile application designed for book lovers who appreciate classic literature. The app provides a dedicated platform for users to browse a curated collection of classics, maintain a personal digital library, and seamlessly order physical copies of their favorite books.

The user experience is centered around simplicity and ease of use, featuring clean navigation, detailed book pages, and a straightforward ordering system. The goal is to create a peaceful and enjoyable environment for discovering and acquiring timeless literary works.

Key Features

  • Secure User Authentication: A safe and simple login system to protect user data and personalize the experience.
  • Interactive Home Page: A welcoming main screen with an auto-rotating image carousel of featured books and curated, scrollable lists.
  • Dynamic Book Browsing: Easily switch between 'Fiction' and 'Non-Fiction' categories to explore the collection, with a simple tap to view details.
  • Seamless Ordering System: A user-friendly form on each book's detail page to order physical copies with validated address and phone number inputs.
  • Store Locator: A dedicated page to find the locations of our physical bookstores.
