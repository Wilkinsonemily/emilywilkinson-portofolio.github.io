Project Overview

TMPOE (Time Management and Productivity Optimization for Education) is a web project that supports learners by providing structured productivity features such as study method exploration, goal-setting, and navigation across learning tools.

The project was developed through a user-centered design process, including prototyping and evaluation, with a focus on making productivity tools simple and accessible.

Key Features

  • Home Page: Overview, feature highlights, and navigation.
  • Study Page: Study methods and productivity systems.
  • Goals Page: Goal-setting and planning workflow.
  • Groups Page: Community discussion and interaction.
  • Help / FAQ: Support page for guidance and common questions.
